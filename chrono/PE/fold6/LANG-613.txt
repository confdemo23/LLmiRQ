ConstructorUtils.getAccessibleConstructor() Does Not Check the Accessibility of Enclosing Classes
ConstructorUtils.getAccessibleConstructor() checks if the declaring class is public but not whether it&amp;apos;s a top-level class or an enclosed one.  Consequently, with enclosed declaring classes, the method does not check if the enclosing class is public, or it&amp;apos;s enclosing class, or it&amp;apos;s enclosing class, etc...
