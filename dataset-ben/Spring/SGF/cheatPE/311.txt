ClientRegionFactoryBean does not properly set the 'concurrency-checks-enabled' attribute and property of GemFire's ClientRegionFactory class.
The Spring Data GemFire ClientRegionFactoryBean does not properly set the 'concurrencyChecksEnabled' property (corresponding to the 'concurrency-checks-enabled' attribute of the &lt;gfe:client-region&gt; element in the SDG XSD) of GemFire's ClientRegionFactory class.
