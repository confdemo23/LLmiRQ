Sets and Integers not appearing in some views.
This is a follow-up to the forum thread:
http://forum.springsource.org/showthread.php?112415-Sets-not-appearing-in-quot-show-quot-views.&amp;p=373337#post373337
The following examples refer to the attached domain objects and fields.
Regarding the fact that some Integers are not displayed: For example a Practice's
formalityLevel,
qualityLevel,
speedLevel,
valueLevel,
perceptionLevel and
outcomeProductionDirectness
are not displayed on the Practices' list.
Regarding the fact that some Sets are not displayed: For example a Risk's Set&lt;OutcomeSufficiency&gt; is correctly displayed on the Risk's "list" view, but not on its "show" view.
