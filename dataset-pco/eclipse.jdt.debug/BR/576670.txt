Add ability to label objects during debugging
It is pretty hard to quickly identify objects and track them during the debugging session, because there could be hundred similar instances, and trying to remember the unique id, is not a too pleasant experience - and the human brain - at least mine - is pretty limited in this area.
 It would be much better, if we could attach label to various objects, and if that object instances show up in the Variables/Expressions view, these labels could be displayed so it will stand out.
