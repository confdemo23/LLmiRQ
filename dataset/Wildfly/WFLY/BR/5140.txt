NoClassDefFoundError in ha configuration
When testing our app with JMeter (5 concurrent users) plenty (hundreds in single request, no kidding) NoClassDefFoundErrors occur. From the client side they are visible as 500 - internal server errors. Unfortunately those errors are not easily reproducible. Not every test run is affected. Actually, I was not able to reproduce them in manual tests, just in JMeter. 
The cluster consists of three servers in HA profile.
