Switching persistence providers from a GAE-based app to a normal JPA app does not clean up special provisions in JavaBean artifacts.
To replicate, create a simple app with GAE/DN persistence and an entity then invoke the peristence setup command and use EclipseLink/HSQL and attempt to compile the app.
