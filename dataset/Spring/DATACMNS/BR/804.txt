IndexOutOfBoundException in ParametersParameterAccessor iterator when non-bindable parameter(s) used
When using a ParametersParameterAccessor on a method with, say, a Pageable (which is non-bindable), attempting to iterate over the accessor throws an IndexOutOfBoundException.
This is because BindableParameterIterator.hasNext() checks values.length, which includes non-bindable parameters, whereas next() calls getBindableValue.
